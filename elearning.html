Why Capture Student Data?

The student database keeps all information together in a single repository so that it is not scattered across teachers and the front office. Because all information is stored together, administrators and teachers can quickly gather the recent and relevant information they need in one place, including class schedules, disciplinary problems, grading, and outstanding payments.
